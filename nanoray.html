NANORAY 900

The Yonex Nanoray 900 (NR900) is a premium, head-light badminton racket favored by advanced players and professional doubles specialists. It is uniquely engineered to suppress the shuttlecock angle upon impact, allowing players to hit incredibly sharp, steep, and aggressive cross-corner smashes and drop shots.

CORE SPECIFICATIONS AND TECHNOLOGIES:

  • BALANCE:Head-Light to Even-Balanced (slightly heavier head feel than traditional Nanospeed models).
  • WEIGHT AND GRIP: Available in 3U (approx. 85g–89g) with G4 or G5 grip sizes.
  • FRAME MATERIAL: H.T. Graphite, H.M. Graphite, Nanometric, and Super HMG.
  • SHAFT MATERIALS: H.M. Graphite, X-Fullerene (minimizes air resistance and increases repulsion power), and Ultra PEF.
  • RECOMMENDED TENSION: 19–24 lbs (3U).
