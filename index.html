ABOUT

FAR BEYOND ORDINARY:

At Yonex, curiosity drives us, and excellence fuels our every move. Founded in 1946 with a vision to bring joy to the world through sport, our legacy is rooted in innovation. Our engineers, designers, and artisans craft world-class sporting equipment that redefines performance and design, always staying true to our mission: to unite the world and deepen the connection between people and the game. We strive to inspire athletes of all levels, from professionals to recreational players, by remaining authentic and approachable in everything we do. Whether refining racquet technology, enhancing footwear, or pioneering new performance solutions, we push the boundaries of possibility. With a perfect blend of master craftsmanship and cutting-edge technology, we create products that empower, transform the game, and drive sport forward for generations to come. Far beyond ordinary.
