NANOFLARE

The Yonex Nanoflare 700 Pro is an elite-tier, head-light badminton racket tailored for rapid swing speeds, defensive play, and front-court control. Featuring a medium flex and a 10mm extended length, it is highly popular among advanced doubles players and strategic all-court singles players.

KEY SPECIFICATIONS:

  • BALANCE:Head-Light
  • WEIGHT AND GRIP SIZE: 4U (80-84g) / G5, G6; 5U available in some regions
  • FRAME MATERIAL:HM Graphite, Nanometric Dr, M40X, Ex-Hyper Mg
  • FLEXIBILITY:Medium
  • RECOMMENDED TENSION: 20–28 lbs (4U) / 19–27 lbs (5U)
