AERUS Z

The Yonex Power Cushion Aerus Z Women's shoe is renowned as the lightest badminton court shoe Yonex produces, weighing just 250 grams per shoe. Designed for maximum speed and agility, it incorporates Power Cushion+ for impact absorption and a Radial Blade Sole for superior court grip.

KEY FEATURES AND TECHNOLOGIES:

  • Feather Light X: A midsole material that keeps the shoe incredibly light while maintaining excellent bounce and repulsion.
  • Double Raschel Mesh & Durable Skin Light: A breathable, seamless mesh upper that reduces weight and prevents the shoe from feeling bulky.
  • 3D Power Graphite Drive: A carbon plate in the midsole that reinforces stability upon landing and aids with propulsion.
  • Synchro-Fit Insole: Ensures a closer, snugger fit between the shoe and the foot to minimize power loss during fast movements.
  • Radial Blade Sole: Provides enhanced grip for multi-directional movements, crucial for indoor sports.
