YONEX CUSHION 90

The "Yonex Power Cushion White" refers to popular, predominantly white colorways of Yonex's signature indoor court and badminton shoes. These shoes are universally renowned for their exceptional shock absorption, lightweight feel, and specialized court traction.

KEY FEATURES OF YONEX CUSHION:

  • POWER CUSHION & POWER CUSHION+: A proprietary shock-absorbing material that absorbs the impact of harsh landings and converts it into repulsion energy for your next step. The enhanced Power Cushion+ offers 28% more shock absorption and 62% more repulsion than standard EVA materials.
  • RADIAL BLADE & HEXA GRIP SOLE: The outsoles are engineered with specialized patterns for aggressive multidirectional traction, letting you lunge and pivot safely without slipping.
  • BREATHABILITY: Uppers frequently feature Double Raschel Mesh to keep your feet cool and ventilated during long, intense rallies.
  • STABLE FOOTWORK:Features like the Power Graphite Sheet and Lateral Shell provide structural support to prevent twisting and reduce power loss during rapid footwork.
