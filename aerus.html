AERUS YONEX

The Yonex Power Cushion Aerus line features Yonex’s lightest badminton shoes, engineered for explosive agility and speed on the court. The blue colorways (such as Cyan, Navy, Mint Blue, or Blue-Gray) are highly popular, weighing roughly 240 to 250 grams per shoe.

CORE TECHNOLOGIES:

  • Feather Light X: A midsole material that makes the Aerus line Yonex's lightest ever, generating faster footwork and repulsion.
  • Power Cushion+: Absorbs shock and transfers impact energy into forward propulsion, making landings softer and more explosive.
  • Radial Blade Sole: Ensures ultimate traction and grip, allowing for quick, confident directional changes during intense rallies.
  • Durable Skin Light: A flexible, rubber-like yet durable polyurethane upper material combined with Double Russel Mesh for high breathability.
  • 3D Power Graphite Sheet: Embedded in the midsole to reinforce stability and reduce twisting when landing.
