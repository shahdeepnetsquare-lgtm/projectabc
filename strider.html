YONEX STRIDER FLOW

The Yonex Power Cushion Strider Flow (and the Strider Flow Wide) are lightweight, agile indoor court shoes designed specifically for badminton and racket sports. Built for aggressive footwork, they focus on shock absorption, stability, and fast, multi-directional movement.

CORE TECHNOLOGIES:

  • POWER CUSHION & POWER CUSHION+: Yonex’s signature shock-absorbing midsole material that converts impact energy into rebound and power for faster footwork.
  • RADIAL BLADE SOLE: An outsole tread pattern designed for exceptional grip in vertical, horizontal, and diagonal directions.
  • ERGOSHAPE: A design that shifts the center of gravity toward the large toe, providing a more comfortable fit while reducing toe pressure.
  • ROUNDSOLE: Provides all-around support to ensure smooth movements and maximum transfer of energy into your next shot.
  • LATERAL SHELL: Reinforcements on the outside of the shoe to prevent foot slippage and reduce power loss during intense sideways movements.
